Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a trip through TravelHub?

You can book a trip by browsing our deals section, selecting your desired package, and following the booking process. You'll need to create an account if you don't already have one.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of booking and the time of cancellation. Generally, cancellations made more than 30 days in advance receive a full refund. Please check the specific terms for your booking.

How can businesses partner with TravelHub?

If you're a business interested in partnering with us, please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@travelhub.com or fill out the contact form on this page selecting "Business Partnerships" as the subject.

How long does it take to get a response from customer support?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our customer support line for immediate assistance.
